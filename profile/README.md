## Flagship Product

### VEIL

**Encrypted on-chain messaging and private payment coordination built on the official Starknet Privacy stack.**

VEIL is designed to support:

- Encrypted on-chain messaging
- Private payment memos
- Confidential offer negotiation
- Private escrow coordination
- Ciphertext-only message discovery

**Current stage:** Pre-production development on Starknet Sepolia.
